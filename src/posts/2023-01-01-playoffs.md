## Week 15

The Red Rifles received a bye, being the Number #1 overall, and number #4 LCD and #5 CHE duked it out to see who made it to the semis. Baskerville and Lockett Down came out of that triumphant at 123 to 110, and progressed.

Meanwhile, #2 BGL got the other bye week, while #3 TBC trounced #6 SKY by 135 to 95, shooting through to the semis.

## Week 16

One tense match, and one not so much.

A rare bad week for Wiggins and RED saw them shamed by LCD in a 138 to 78 pounding (diff 60 pts) The end of a dream for the ginger gladiators.

Meanwhile, in a much closer game, BGL pipped TBC to the post to make it into the finals in a 117.16 to 116.66 nailbiter (diff 0.50)

## To the bowl!

Ergo Lockett Down (LCD) and Beginners Luck (BGL) progress to the Fantasy Super Bowl. Good luck!
