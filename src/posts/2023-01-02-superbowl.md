*Ed note - backfilled this post at start of 2023/24 season*

Congratulations to this years' champions Lockett Down (LCD) and their coach/manager Tom Baskerville!

LCD met Naisbitt's side Beginners Luck (BGL) in the final and led throughout the game, getting ahead early and staying there through Sunday night.

Despite a huge 36 pt performance from QB D. Jones, BGL couldn't keep up, as they suffered at wide receiver and flex. Baskerville's side put in an even performance across the team, with a standout defensive show courtesy of the Jaguars' line.


| Lockett Down | Beginners Luck
|--------|--------
| 111.88 | 104.78

Congrats again to our 2022/23 champs!

## Consolation

Overcoming last week's humilation, the Red Rifles came back with aplomb and gutted TBC by a whopping 150 to 65.
