Standing alone at 3-0, newcomer Tom B and his side ‘Cannot touch my Williams’ (CTW) enjoyed the lowest winning score of the week (94.90), as it catapulted them to first place in the league.

In fact, CTW would hover in only 7th place overall based on overall points scored, with The Pollard Express (TPE) topping that particular table. Those two teams will eventually meet in week 7, and in the meantime, it remains to be seen whether Baskerville’s side can continue their streak without a game score topping 113.24.

## Marriage Poison Week

With fresh determination after last weeks loss in the Sibling Bowl, Grace and Country Love (CCL) triumphed in the marital matchup, defeating Bryn’s Brady Bunch (TBB) a handy 128 to 86. Their first win of the season takes them to just 11th whilst husband languishes winless in 13th place.
