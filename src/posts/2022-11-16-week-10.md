*Editor's note - there was no week 9 report as the staff of TPC were in transit to Argentina.*

It was a low-scoring week in every matchup but one, where Trebble's CRT faced Joel and BGL, the teams scoring the two highest totals of the weekend. The Homey Roaders took the win: 176.78 to 148.28

The Skylarks (SKY), Cheetahs (CHE), and Categoricals (CCL) all won their matchups. The narrowest margin went to the Skylarks, as their opponents in the Brady Bunch (BRR) scored disappointingly, and couldn't be saved by Jalen Hurts on Monday Night, as he hit his prediction but did not exceed the 25.8 needed to win. Incidentally, Bryn and Ste both played their optimal lineups this week.

The Rifles (RED) continue their impervious streak and go to 8-2.

LCD scored a decent 114.6 and the Collies (ESQ) couldn't muster a response.

Likewise, TBC won with 111.24 to the 82.76 put forth by PFC. The latter fielded Renfrow on IR and Zuerlein on Bye. Meadows has three defences and an empty bench slot -- sort it out, man!!

A clearer playoff picture is emerging as the top three in RED, CHE, and LCD look set to go through. Then there are around nine teams in the hunt with records ranging from 6-4 to 4-6, MFB having the highest mountain to climb. Playing for pride are our go-getter underdogs in Hubble's BRR and Meadows' PFC.
