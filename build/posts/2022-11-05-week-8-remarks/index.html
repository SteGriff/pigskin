Week 8 - Remarks

  • Wow! The RED Rifles are really solid, huh? They won again and retain the highest record in the league.

  • TBC were highest scorers at 189.66 thanks to Kamara (42.8) and Waddle (30.6)

  • NAP Bearcats took a windfall victory win over BGL.

  • CHE scored a huge 162.14 and beat CCL, even though Zeke brought in a doughnut (0)... Hill, Hopkins, and Tagavailoa were all in the 30 pt ballpark. Naz picked up Hopkins as a free agent quite a few weeks before his suspension was up. It proved to be a savvy move.

  • Jonny didn't trust his Commanders-Receivers at the Colts; a bad call, as they put up 27.5 while his active WR and Flex scored just 8 pts in total. Tom and LCD won the matchup, acheiving their highest score of the season so far at 135.10.

  • Depth at RB is now so bad that you will routinely see coaches splashing out 20% of their season budget on, like, RB #56.

  • The editor retains an inexplicable faith that PFC will win at least one game before the season is over.
